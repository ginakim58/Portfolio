So, Who Am I?

Computer Science Student

I’m a senior at California State University, Fullerton, set to graduate in December 2024. Over the past three years, I’ve immersed myself in coding, working with languages like C++, C#, Python, and JavaScript. I’ve also explored various electives to discover my interests and define the career path I plan to pursue after graduation.

Recently, I’ve been actively working on several projects, including my senior capstone, AI-driven applications, and a platform game. These experiences have sharpened my technical skills and deepened my passion for solving real-world problems through programming.

Among the languages I’ve worked with, I’m most comfortable with C++ and Python. I began my coding journey with C++, which built a strong foundation, and later picked up Python in my second year, appreciating its simplicity and versatility.

Outside of coding, I enjoy playing guitar, exploring video games, and creating DIY projects with Raspberry Pis. These hobbies let me balance creativity with problem-solving and keep me energized both in and out of the tech world.
